Our Amazing Owners

Emporer Sheev Palpatine

Owner

Emporer Sheev Palpatine has humble origins beginning on the planet Naboo. He started there with humble origins and quickly rose in prominence to the rank of Galactic Senator. In this role, he helped Naboo prosper and succeed before taking the role of Chancellor at the onset of the Clone Wars. Although attempting to help bring peace to the galaxy, he was attacked by a band of jedi and left to rule the galaxy.

Lord Darth Vader

General Manager

Born into slavery on the desert planet of Tatooine, Darth Vader has always wanted to explore the galaxy. From the time he was a podracing child, he knew that he wanted to go fast and see what was beyond the stars. He served as part of the jedi order and helped uncover their evil plot to take over the Galactic Senate. He helped Emporer Palpatine defeat the evil jedi, so he could help rule the galaxy. Priding himself on experience, let him know if there is anything that could make your stay better.

Our Stupendous Staff

Storm Troopers

General Staff

Born in the wake of the first Galactic Empire, the storm trooper staff members rose to the occasion upon the retirement of the republic clone troopers. These storm troopers are known for their cunning ability to meet the needs of all who they serve. No boundary is off limits, and they hope to make your stay as enjoyable as possible.
