Our Amazing Owners

Emporer Sheev Palpatine

Owner

Emporer Sheev Palpatine has humble origins beginning on the planet Naboo during the Republic Era. He quickly rose in prominence to the rank of Galactic Senator. In this role, he helped Naboo prosper and succeed before taking the role of Supreme Chancellor at the onset of the invasion of Naboo as well as the Clone Wars. Although attempting to help bring peace to the galaxy, he was attacked by a band of jedi and was forced to solve the issue. He created the New Order with himself as the Galactic Emporer, a title he continues to hold as he creates a galaxy of the future.

Lord Darth Vader

General Manager

Rising from the failures of Anakin Skywalker, Darth Vader is one of the strongest rulers known to the galaxy. Upon the revealing of the evil jedi plot, Vader protected Emporer Palpatine and helped with the formation of the New Order. As general manager of the Death Star, Vader continues his mission to bring peace and prosperity to the galaxy one planet at a time. Priding himself on guest experience, let him know if there is anything that could make your stay better.

Our Stupendous Staff

Storm Troopers

General Staff

Born in the wake of the New Order, the storm trooper staff members rose to the occasion upon the retirement of the republic clone troopers. These storm troopers are known for their cunning ability to meet the needs of all who they serve. No boundary is off limits, and they hope to make your stay as enjoyable as possible.
